Stroke Rehabilitation

Caregiving After Stroke

Providing care post-stroke can be overwhelming. Caregivers often lack necessary skills and support, needing to adapt to the patient's illness and navigate unmet home care needs identified in studies.

Navigating Insurance

Understanding health insurance coverage is crucial for managing hospitalization and rehabilitation costs post-stroke. Coverage limitations and out-of-pocket expenses should be clarified to anticipate changes as the patient's condition progresses.

Supporting Stroke Rehabilitation

Active involvement in stroke rehabilitation is beneficial. Encourage participation in therapy sessions to aid recovery while promoting independence. Assess and meet the survivor's evolving needs through medical team consultations and home adjustments.

  • Encourage therapy sessions for recovery
  • Assess needs, adjust home with medical team guidance
