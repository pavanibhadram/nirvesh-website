Music Therapy

Benefits of Music on Brain Health

Listening to music can enhance mood, reduce stress, improve focus, and manage anxiety and depression. It boosts cognitive performance, aids in pain management, enhances memory, and promotes productivity and endurance.

Music Therapy in Stroke Treatment

Music activates brain structures crucial for sensory processing, attention, memory, and cognition. Musical training benefits auditory and audiovisual processing, aiding stroke rehabilitation. It enhances subcortical and cortical brain regions, facilitating recovery and sensorimotor functions.

Music Therapy in Brain Injury

Neurologic Music Therapy (NMT) optimizes sensorimotor, language, and cognitive functions through tailored music interventions. It improves executive functions, emotional regulation, and reduces depression and anxiety post-TBI. Music-based therapies also aid movement rehabilitation, reduce agitation, and enhance adaptive behaviors in both adult and pediatric populations.

  • Enhances executive functions and emotional regulation
  • Aids movement rehabilitation and enhances adaptive behaviors
